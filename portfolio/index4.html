My first structure is the Sonic. Sonic is one of the icons in SEGA game company. When I was a child, I played Dreamcast with my cousin. It is such a memorable thing for me. Overall, it looks identified and cool but there are some parts of it messed up. And I realize the cause is that there is nothing to support the fallen part. In the second print, I make a Subaru emblem with positive and negative part. Based on the experience I got, it won't be tailored if I print the whole emblem out. This is why I print it separately as positive part and negative part. And I do the paint job and glue them. I really enjoy the processing of making it, and I will put it in my car as an icon of making 3D print.
